Version 1 (September 2021)

Support for multiple digital healthcare credential standards

Digital Health Pass supports emerging digital record standards that are used to identify vaccination status for individuals. These standards encourage interoperability among providers, while maintaining security and privacy. The Digital Health Pass Wallet and Digital Health Pass Verify mobile apps now support these credentials and their QR codes.

Supported healthcare credential types:

  • Digital Health Pass

  • Digital COVID Certificate (European Union)

  • SMART Health Cards (VCIâ„¢)

  • Good Health Pass

For more information, see the Digital Health Pass Administration Guide. To access it in the administration console, click Help and then select Administration Guide.

Verifier metrics

System Administrators and Customer Administrators can review verification-related statistics for a selected Digital Health Pass customer or organization. Easy-to-understand donut charts summarize verifier activity by credential type, scan results, and issuer for a specified time range.
